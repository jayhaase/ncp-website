# Nature Connected Professionals

## Organization Mission
The Nature-Connected Professionals gather to cultivate depth through community building, engage broadly through networking, and steward care for life by generously sharing skills, knowledge, and the wisdom of experience.

## Website
- The website should feel beautiful, grounded, and professional, with easy-to-read content and clear, upfront information about services.
- Inspiring but not intimidating, practical and straightforward, and not flashy or confusing to navigate.
- Highly accessible
- Intentionally low cognitive load
- No parallax
- No scrolling tricks



### Website Content
#### About the group  
1. What this group is, who it’s for, and why it exists, in clear, accessible language.
2. Simple “how this works” overview (seasonal gatherings, unconference style, etc.).

#### Events and Gatherings
1. Seasonal Gathering info.
2. Unconference details.
3. Group calendar where people can see upcoming events and add their own.

#### Connect with Others
1. Directory of members.
2. Slack / online community info and how to join.
3. Encouragement and clear calls to action to participate and reach out.

#### Join and Participate
- Could be its own area or woven into the others.
- Simple steps to get involved, sign up, or become part of the directory / Slack / events.
